POLICY UPDATE: Grade Level Funding Eligibility
Effective Date: 2024-2025 School Year

Starting in the 2024-2025 school year, funding calculations will change based on student grade level:

1. Elementary students (K-2): Full per-pupil funding allocation
2. Primary students (3-5): Per-pupil funding + 10% performance bonus if proficiency rate >= 70%
3. Middle school students (6-8): Per-pupil funding + 15% performance bonus if proficiency rate >= 75%
4. High school students (9-12): Per-pupil funding + 20% performance bonus if graduation rate >= 85%

This means that the dashboard must now:
- Calculate proficiency rates separately by grade band
- Apply different multipliers to funding calculations
- Track graduation rates for 9-12 grade cohorts specifically

Questions for clarification:
- Does this apply retroactively to 2023-24 data?
- How are students counted who move grades mid-year?
