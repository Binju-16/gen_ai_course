POLICY UPDATE: Special Population Performance Tracking
Effective Date: Start of Next Academic Year

New requirement: All dashboard reports must now separately track proficiency metrics for special populations:

1. English Language Learners (ELL)
   - Track proficiency rate for ELL students
   - Must disaggregate by year of ELL status (Year 1, Year 2+)

2. Students with Individualized Education Plans (IEP)
   - Track proficiency rate for IEP students
   - Compare to general education proficiency rate

3. Students Receiving Free/Reduced Lunch (FRL)
   - Track proficiency rate for FRL students
   - Track FRL percentage of total enrollment

Funding impact: Schools with > 30% ELL population receive 8% additional per-pupil funding to support instruction.

Dashboard changes needed:
- Add demographic-based proficiency calculations
- Add demographic-based funding calculations
- Create new report sections for each subgroup
